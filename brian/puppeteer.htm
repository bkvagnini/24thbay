This was written about a woman named Mercy, who I was on tour with. I liked her and when I explained how I felt, she told me that she doesn't date people that she works with. Fair enough. However, she soon took a liking to the guitar player and began seeing him. This song starts off with my feelings and ends with her being in control (not of me but of him, you see...). I remember telling my roommate towards the end of the tour, as he was hauling not only his bags, but hers as well (kinda looked like a pack mule) "That would've been me!" Sometimes things work out for the best. Interesting postscript...They ended up marrying each other a short time later and to my knowledge, they are still married as of 2002. Strange the way things work out...
